### Bingley 26 Weekend - June 6th/7th

The [Bingley 26 Weekend](https://www.bingleyartscentre.co.uk/events/bingley-26-weekend) on June 6th/7th 2026 see the official start of the [Summer Bandstand programme in Myrtle Park]({% link _venues/myrtle_park.md %}) BUT don't forget the Salvation Army Band will be there on May 24th, and Vinyl Revival will be playing records on May 30th.

Follow the [Myrtle Park page]({% link _venues/myrtle_park.md %}) on our website or just keep and eye on the [live listings]({% link live.md %}).

#### Can you help at the Bandstand?

We're looking for people to:
* Look after the performers at each event
* Talk to the audience to tell them about the event and other stuff
* plug in the electricity and PA
* help people with the chairs
* look after the crowd (stewards)
* count the numbers

Contact info@bingleymusictown.org.uk if you'd like to help or come and see us at an event.

### Music in our Parish

Bingley Parish Church has lots of music coming up.

There's the Ale Saints Beer Festival with Live Music on both days on Friday 22nd and Saturday 23rd May from (22nd) 4-9pm (23rd) 1-9pm. Cost - £8 on the door (in church house, old Main Street) 

Dolly Mixtures and Madrigalis on Saturday 6th June at 6pm. £10 (includes tea/coffee and cake). Tickets available - on the door or via the parish office

#### Organ Recitals: 
An exciting set of Organ Recitals on the recently refurbished church organ take place on Wednesdays in June (and one in July).

The organ was originally built in 1883 for Sion Baptist Chapel in Bradford but was moved to All Saints Church in the early 1960s when new a some additional stops were added including a 'zimbalstern'! The organ is large and rather powerful especially for a 'parish church' and so is rather desirable to play. 
 
* 3rd June Geoffrey Wollacott (Bradford Cathedral) 
* 10th June Martin Baker (St Oswald's, Guiseley) 
* 17th June Michael Pratt (Menston Methodist) 
* 24th June Ed Jones (freelance organist) 
* 1st July Anthony Gray (St. Wilfred's, Harrogate) 

Time: 7pm, Free - donations welcome  

Later in the year you can see [Alchemy Live (Dire Straits Tribute)](https://www.tickettailor.com/events/allsaintschurch1/2129438), [City of Bradford Brass Band](https://www.tickettailor.com/events/allsaintschurch1/2167589), [Floyd in the flesh (Pink Floyd Tribute Act)](https://buytickets.at/allsaintschurch1/1946743) - tickets from [https://www.tickettailor.com/events/allsaintschurch1](https://www.tickettailor.com/events/allsaintschurch1)

### Grassroots music settles into Bingley Arts Centre 

Both [Bingley First Monday Folk](https://www.facebook.com/groups/1823602681007824/user/61566637350036) and [Rumble](https://rumbleevents.co.uk) are settling into their new homes at the [Bingley Arts Centre](www.bingleyartscentre.co.uk). Check out any of the their facebook pages and websites to see what's on.

[Next up at the Rumble](https://rumbleevents.co.uk/events/may-2026) is [David Cronenberg's Wife](https://davidcronenbergswife.bandcamp.com/) +  (all the way from London, part of their UK tour and they are stopping off to see us!) and [fig by four](https://figbyfour.bandcamp.com/)

### Live at the Chip 'N' Ern

The [upstairs space at the Chip 'N' Ern](https://chip.rumbleevents.co.uk) is now getting regular use as a live music venue. There's a [new website](https://chip.rumbleevents.co.uk) where you can easily see what's on AND you can book the space if you want to play there.
